About Us

Nestled in a cozy corner of Little Lonsdale Street, Bakemono is home to a snack selection of carefully curated pastries, featuring tall, puffy Danishes bursting with flavors like blueberries and custard, Nutella and jammy berries, or apple and stewed rhubarb. Indulge in our almond croissants infused with a citrusy kick from yuzu, alongside classic ham and cheese croissants. Don't miss our canelés, delicate French cakes with a hint of rum, or our soft, fluffy loaves of shokupan, Japanese milk bread baked fresh at midday. Our perpetually sold-out garlic bread, a beloved Korean street food, is a must-try. Made with the same dough as our shokupan, it's baked into dome-shaped buns, filled with slightly sweet cream cheese, dunked in a bowl of butter and garlic, then baked to perfection. Inspired by cafes and bakeries in Japan and Korea, our design is a collaborative effort. Large windows invite you inside, adorned with timber paneling, while the interior boasts crisp white walls accented with pine furniture. A potted Japanese maple graces the center, its sparse orange leaves adding a touch of warmth. Unlike our sister spots, Rogue and Shamble Coffee Brewers, which source beans from Acoffee, we proudly serve Small Batch coffee. Our hot chocolate is crafted by Mörk, and chai lattes are brewed in-house weekly. Bakemono also supplies pastries to Slater Street Bench, 580 Bench, and Acoffee, spreading the joy of artisanal treats across Melbourne.
